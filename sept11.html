The biggest reasons for the difference is how specific you need to be. div is good if your working with a chunk of things in one spot, or need to section something of from everything else. Span is good for inline modifications. id is used when you have one thing and one thing only. class is best when you have multiple things throughout the sheet that you want styled the same.
